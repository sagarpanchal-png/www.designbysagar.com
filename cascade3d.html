Overview

The Cascade 3D mobile app is a healthcare platform designed to support elderly and vulnerable individuals in their homes using IoT technology.

Role

UI/UX Designer & UX Researcher

Duration

8 weeks (February 2023 - April 2023)

Tools

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Xd, Lottie files

The Problem

The challenge was to create a user-friendly mobile app that integrates with IoT devices, maintains health records, sends real-time alerts, and adheres to accessibility standards while delivering cost-effective and personal care solutions. We aimed to address the challenges caregivers often face with complex healthcare apps.

Solution

Delivered a mobile app design that was intuitive, user-friendly, and complied with accessibility standards. The app offered seamless IoT integration, providing real-time data and alerts to users, contributing to better and personalized care.
