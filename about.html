Overview

Cascade Connected Care is a digital healthcare platform that employs IoT technology to support vulnerable and elderly individuals.

Role

UI/UX Designer and UX Researcher

Duration

3 months (February 2023 - April 2023)

Tools

Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Xd, Lottie files

The Problem

Our task was to design a mobile application that would seamlessly integrate with the Cascade platform, providing caregivers with real-time updates and insights while adhering to stringent accessibility standards. We aimed to address the challenges caregivers often face with complex healthcare apps.

Design process

Solution

We prototyped the Cascade 3D Mobile Application and iteratively tested it for usability and experience. Following its launch, the app has been adopted by over 1,000 caregivers, affirming its effectiveness in providing real-time behavioral insights.

Reflections

The Cascade project underscored the significance of understanding user requirements and effectively addressing them through thoughtful design. Our user feedback has been positive, confirming that our focus on real-time updates and accessibility has significantly improved their efficiency.
